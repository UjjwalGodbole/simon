Simon Game

Rules to play

  1. Start the Game: Press any key on your keyboard to begin the game. The game will display the first color sequence.
  2. Watch the Sequence: Carefully watch the sequence of colors displayed. The game will show a sequence of lights that you need to remember.
  3. Repeat the Sequence: Click on the colored boxes in the same order as the sequence shown. Each round, the sequence will get longer by one additional color.
  4. Follow the Order: Ensure you click the colors in the exact order as presented. If you click a color out of order, the game will end.
  5. Challenge Your Memory: Try to remember and repeat the longest sequence possible without making a mistake. The goal is to achieve the highest sequence length you can!
